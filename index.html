Cyber Crime Awareness

Stay informed, stay safe!

What is Cyber Crime?

Cybercrime refers to criminal activities carried out by means of computers or the internet. It can involve everything from identity theft to hacking and the spread of malware.


Why do we need the awareness of cybercrimes?

Cybercrime awareness is important because cybercrime can have a devastating impact on individuals and businesses, and because many major data breaches are caused by human error: Cost: Cybercrime is estimated to cost the world $10.5 trillion annually by 2025. Impact on individuals: Cybercrime can lead to identity theft, financial losses, and reputational damage. Impact on businesses: Cybercrime can lead to loss of customer trust and financial impact on companies. Human error: Many major data breaches are caused by human error.


Viruses

Viruses are malicious software programs designed to spread from one computer to another. They can corrupt data, steal information, and disrupt system operations.
